• The New Logo
  • The New Tagline
With friendly curves and inviting colors, our new logo represents Phoebe’s support for the community and celebrates the balance between ongoing innovation and patient-centered healthcare.

Innovation

“A Smart Balance to Progress” The dynamic, growing but balanced shapes demonstrate our practice of always evaluating, innovating, and growing in our approach to delivering better care.

Collaboration, System-ness

“Cycle of Care” Represents the continuum of care, demonstrating how Phoebe supports patients at all stages of life and all levels of care. Also reflects the longevity of serving the health and wellness needs of our community for more than 100 years.

Involved, Patient-centered Care

“Compassionate Embrace” Overlapping swirls of color represent our close, symbiotic relationship with patients, embracing and understanding their needs and delivering compassionate care that supports our community’s overall wellness. Blue denotes trust and knowledge while green conveys health, newness, and a nod to Phoebe’s visual history.

Expert and Trustworthy

“Advanced and Approachable” The warm grey tone and cross-strokes of the font provide interest and a subtle nod to the hospital cross symbol while maintaining readability. We made the font even more distinct and ownable by adding subtle curvature to complement the mark.

Better

A promise to always strive to create new, innovative and smarter ways to deliver quality care that makes – and keeps – our patients well. It also speaks to a higher level of compassion for patients and for our Phoebe family.

Way

Speaks to our foresight and planning – an intentional path and long range vision to create the best healthcare solution for our community. Emphasizes that we’re focused on delivering more than just care for illness and sickness we’re invested in and dedicated to making our communities healthier and happier for a lifetime of overall wellness.
